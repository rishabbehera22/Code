KARAN SHAW Founder

EXFIN solution Private Limited is headed by Karan Shaw, he is the fellow member of Institute of Chartered Accountant of India (ICAI) and Associate member of Institute of Cost Accountant of India (ICMAI) by profession, having around 10 years of expertise in the field of Accountancy, Banking and Finance. He has started his career by joining one of the Multi National Company named “GENPACT”, where he was entrusted to head group of professional accountants to oversee the accounting needs of the Overseas client. After serving there, he joined HDFC BANK as Credit Manager at Kolkata Head Office. There he was engaged in providing credit facilities to the clients towards unsecured business loan, Loan against property and Corporate Loan and gained in depth knowledge in due diligence. With the vision to do something big for himself and to utilize the exposure in gained through his journey, he started his own Chartered Accountant Firm in the name of K SHAW AND ASSOCIATES based out at Raniganj, West Bengal and within the short span of time he expanded the operation by opening various branch at Dehri Onsne, Aurangabad, Gaya, Patna and Ranchi.
