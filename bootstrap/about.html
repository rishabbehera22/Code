BRIEF BACKGROUND ABOUT THE COMPANY It’ll blow your mind.

THIS UNIT WAS PROMOTED AND ESTABLISHED IN THE YEAR 1969 BY SHRI JAGDISH KUMAR BEHERA, A GRADUATE MECHANICAL ENGEENER.
THIS ENGINEERING UNIT HAS SPECIALISATION IN ALLTPES OF HEAT TREATMENT, EQUIPMENT & STRUCTURAL FABRICATION INCLUDING PRECISION MACHINING. THIS SSI UNIT HAS THE ADVANTAGE OF ALL THIS FACILITIES UNDER ONE COMPLEX. OUR HEAT TREATMENT FURNACES AND ITS ACCESSOARIES HAVE BEEN CERTIFIED BY Lloyd’s Register of Shipping & EXTERNAL CALIBRATING AGENCY. THE 50 MT. FURANCE IS A SEMI AUTOMATIC BOGIE HEARTH OIL FIRED FURANCE. THE ONLY ONE OF ITS KIND AND CAPACITY WITH CONTROLS AVAILABLE IN EASTERN INDIA.
